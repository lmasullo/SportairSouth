About us

SportairSouth, LLC is a dealership representing SportairUSA, LC.

Our role is to introduce you to the many fine Light Sport Aircraft available from SportairUSA and answer any questions. We have a Sting S3 based in Lampasas, TX that you can come check out and demo. We can arrange for you to meet with Bill Canino of SportairUSA and demo any of the other available aircraft based in North Little Rock, AR.


SportairUSA, LC, has been serving the aviation community since 1990. It is an FAA certified Repair Station and the North American distributor for aircraft manufactured by TL-Ultralight, s.r.o., and Zlin Aviation, s.r.o. These aircraft include the best-selling Sting, a low wing, carbon-fiber composite sport plane; the iCub, a light sport version of the enduring Super Cub; and the Sirius, a high wing companion to the Sting. The airplanes are constructed, flight-tested, then disassembled at the factory and shipped in ocean containers to SportairUSAâ€™s Arkansas facility where they are reassembled, individually flight-tested and FAA certified by our Designated Airworthiness Representative (DAR). SportairUSA adds avionics and other components to meet the requirements of the American market, and delivers them to our dealers and customers. SportairUSA conducts parts warehousing, shipping and receiving, transition flight training, customer support and marketing out of its central Arkansas facilities along with the assembly, certification and distribution activities described above. We maintain an inventory of parts for maintenance and repair of these aircraft in order to support our customers. SportairUSA also distributes the SeaRey, a popular amphibious seaplane that is manufactured by Progressive Aerodyne, Inc., in Orlando, Florida.
